IT Strategy Last updated: 2019-06-01

IT Strategy- can be a long-term and short-term plan for achieving a goal in the fast-changing technology field. For example, a small company using a WordPress platform to host their online store, but it is feasible to use WordPress when the business grows bigger in 3-5 years.

Business Strategy

is planning and decision management makes in an organization to reach its goal and be competitive in the market at the same time.

Business-IT Strategy Alignment

“Business-IT alignment is a process in which a business organization uses information technology (IT) to achieve business objectives - typically improved financial performance or marketplace competitiveness.” In a simple term, Information technology is used as tools to fix, manage, and to achieve the goal.

Business Model

“A business model describes the rationale of how an organization creates, delivers, and captures value, in economic, social, cultural or other contexts.” Starting any new business should have a business model to be successful. The business model includes design a product and raw materials, marketing, and sales to price and payment methods.

Value Creation

“is performance of actions that increase the worth of goods, services, or even a business.”

Digital Strategy

is a part of business strategy. In digital strategy, the use of social media platforms, data modeling, and data analysis are used to market and target the right consumers.

Value Chain

“ is a business model that describes the full range of activities needed to create a product or service.” The activities process include from the start to finish and provide a visual model of business to align with the competitors.

IT Policy

IT Policy- “the organization defines rules, regulations, and guidelines for proper usage and maintenance of these technological assets to ensure their ethical and acceptable use and assure health, safety and security of data, products, facilities as well as the people using them.”
